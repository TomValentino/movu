# PROPERTY KIT — BRAND SPEC

## THE COMPANY
Real estate growth platform. Suite: premium website/funnel builder, managed paid ads (Meta/Google/YouTube), SEO + content, AI lead nurture, branded homeowner dashboard. Not self-serve SaaS — high-touch service with AI underneath.

---

## THE CLIENT
Top 10% of real estate producers by volume. Multi–7-figure net worth. Tier-1 and luxury/coastal markets. Reference brands: Apple, Porsche, Four Seasons, Hermès. They know quality — and they know when it's missing. They buy outcomes, not features. Their pain: a digital presence that doesn't match their real-world reputation.

---

**"Built for real estate's top performers."**

## EVERY DEVICE
The platform's end-users — homeowners, buyers, sellers — are overwhelmingly on mobile. Every design decision must hold up at 400px just as well as 1280px. Not "responsive enough." Actually beautiful on every screen. This is non-negotiable for a brand that sells premium digital presence to agents whose own clients will judge them on it.

---

## THE TEST

> *"Would a $5M/year real estate agent be proud to have their name on this?"*

Apply to every decision — not just copy:

- **Layout:** Does it feel spacious and considered, or rushed and cluttered?
- **Color:** Does it feel warm and premium, or generic and template-like?
- **Typography:** Does the hierarchy feel intentional and editorial, or default and lazy?
- **Components:** Does every element feel designed for this brand specifically?
- **Interactions:** Do hover states and transitions feel weighted and deliberate?
- **Mobile:** Does it hold up on a phone screen — or does it only shine on desktop?

If the answer to any of these is "no" — refine it. The client notices everything, even if they can't name it.
